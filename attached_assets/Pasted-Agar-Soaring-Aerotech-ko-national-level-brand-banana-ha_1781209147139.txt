# Biggest Change Needed

### Current Feel

"Drone Training & Survey Company"

### Desired Feel

"National Drone Technology, Research & Manufacturing Organization"

Ye difference website ke design se nahi, **positioning** se aata hai.

Agar homepage par sirf courses aur services dikhoge to training institute lagoge.

Agar homepage par:

* Projects
* Clients
* Manufacturing
* Research
* Innovation
* Government Collaborations

dikhoge to national-level drone ecosystem company lagoge.

Aur brochure dekhkar sach kahun to Soaring ke paas content already hai — website ko bas us scale ko properly showcase karna hai.
